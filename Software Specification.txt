Built Coffee Bean Trust - a blockchain-verified supply chain traceability platform showing coffee's journey from farm to cup with certifications, quality scores, and transparent tracking.

Key Features:

Consumer interface to track coffee from farm to cup
Interactive supply chain journey visualization
Certification management (organic, fair trade, quality scores)
Real-time batch tracking with blockchain verification
Beautiful, trustworthy design with coffee aesthetics
Design Language:

Warm earth tones: rich browns, coffee cream, forest greens, gold accents
Premium, sophisticated feel like high-end coffee brands
Smooth animations showing bean journey
Mobile-optimized for in-store scanning
App Structure:

Entities: Coffee batches, supply chain events, certifications, participants
Pages: Consumer search/lookup, detailed batch journey view
Components: Timeline visualization, certification badges, participant cards, trust indicators

Added Trace cobsole, Analytics console, and Certifications console with navigation buttons - now users can view full journey, explore supply chain analytics, and check certificates from both home page and batch details.
